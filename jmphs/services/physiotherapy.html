Physiotherapy

  • Identify the root cause of pain and inflammation in the musculoskeletal system.
  • Encourage the bodies natural healing process, by muscular, joint, and nervous system manipulation.
  • Restore, and teach normal movement patterns to reduce the risk of recurrence.

Exercise therapy

John can help advise you on specific exercises to maximise recovery and health, without aggravating pain. He will assist in finding incorrect or inefficient technique in physical activities, by analysing the bodies natural movement patterns.

Exercise therapy involves identifying specific weaknesses or imbalances in the body. John will help to find the underlying cause of the pain, and help to find a suitable road to improvement.

Pain management

John can help manage pain by finding root causes of imbalances in the body, and offering a proper treatment plan.

Feet

John believes feet are the solid base, that are often neglected and cause many problems which go unaddressed. He can help answer concerns and offer a studious opinion.

Movement training

Eastern medicine believe your real age is based on the health of your spine. It is the quality of segmental movement that is paramount. The ’weak core’ theory has recently been questioned by research. The science of movement training is to gain segmented control over the body, with correct patterning.
