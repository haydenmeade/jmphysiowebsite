Medicare

Medicare does cover up to five private physiotherapy services a year with a correct referral from your doctor.

HICAPS

Our practice offers the convenience of HICAPS for fast, on the spot claiming through your Private Health Cover provider. Just bring your private health card along with you.

Workcover / Transport Accident Commision Patients

Workcover or Transport Accident Commission patients should indicate their position to the receptionist at the time of making the appointment. Following determination of liability, the insurers will be billed directly. Patients are reminded however that they are responsible for their payments should liability be denied.

Veterans’ Affairs

Veterans’ Affairs patients will have their services billed directly to Veteran Affairs. A current medical referral is required before liability is accepted.

Emergencies

Should you require treatment or advice urgently outside clinic hours, contact John on his mobile number.

Home visits

The clinic offers the service of a home visit for those who are unable to come to the rooms for themselves.
